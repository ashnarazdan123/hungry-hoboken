About Us

Who is Hungry Hoboken?

The goal of Hungry Hoboken is to provide a website that gives people the ability to search for food to order in their area. We believe that there are not great options for people with dietary restrictions when using apps and websites to find food to order out. Using the website we are designing, patrons will be able to search through a variety of Hoboken restaurants sorted based on their dietary restrictions.
